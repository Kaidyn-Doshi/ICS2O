To change the wallpaper on a Mac, follow these simple steps:

1. Click on the Apple logo in the top left corner of the screen.
2. Choose "System Preferences" from the drop-down menu.
3. Click on "Desktop and Screen Saver".
4. Click on the "Desktop" tab to see your current wallpaper.
5. Click on the "+" button in the left corner to add a new picture as your wallpaper.
6. Choose a picture from your computer and click "Open".
7. Your new picture will now be set as your wallpaper.
